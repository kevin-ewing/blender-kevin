Nodevember

Nodevember is all about procedural awesomeness! During the month of November I challenged myself to improve and show off my procedural skills. The constraints I set for myself:

Shading/texturing:
  • No image textures.
  • No nodes that load external data.

Modelling:
  • No manual sculpting/modelling.
  • Basic primitives only.
  • No nodes that load external data.

Enjoy!
